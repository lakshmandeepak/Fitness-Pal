Here is your Personalized Workout Plan!!


Day 1: Full-Body Strength Training

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, brisk walking)
Strength Training:
Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps each leg
Bent-over rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
Cool Down and Stretch: Spend 5-10 minutes stretching major muscle groups.



Day 2: Cardio and Core

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or cycling
Cardio Workout:
Interval training (sprint for 1 minute, walk/jog for 2 minutes) for 20-30 minutes
Core Exercises:
Planks: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds
Russian twists: 3 sets of 15 reps
Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps each side
Cool Down and Stretch: Stretch core muscles and legs.



Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

Rest Day: Allow your body to recover. Light activities like walking or yoga can be beneficial.



Day 4: Upper Body Strength

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of dynamic stretching
Upper Body Strength Workout:
Bench press or push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
Dumbbell shoulder press: 3 sets of 12 reps
Pull-ups or lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Tricep dips: 3 sets of 12 reps
Cool Down and Stretch: Focus on upper body stretches.



Day 5: Cardio and Lower Body

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
Cardio Workout:
Running, cycling, or elliptical for 30-40 minutes at moderate intensity
Lower Body Exercises:
Deadlifts or hip thrusts: 3 sets of 10 reps
Leg press or bodyweight squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
Calf raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
Cool Down and Stretch: Stretch lower body muscles.



Day 6: Active Rest or Flexibility Training

Yoga or Pilates: Focus on flexibility and mobility exercises to improve range of motion and reduce muscle tension.



Day 7: Outdoor Activity or Active Rest

Outdoor Activity: Hiking, swimming, or any enjoyable physical activity to stay active and engaged.
